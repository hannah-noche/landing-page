What is Horizon?

Horizon is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the education and support of Filipinos.

We fund our activities through private donations, government grants, partnerships with companies and international organisations, and fundraising events.

We also work closely with local authorities and schools to maximise our impact and ensure continued support.

Our goals

Horizon aims to open up new opportunities for Filipinos by giving them the language skills they need to succeed in an increasingly globalised world.

We offer English, French, Spanish, Arabic and much more through a pen pal programme.
